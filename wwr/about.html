WildForest Rafting About us

History

Our company was founded in 1999, when a group of friends passionate about the rivers of southern Chile decided to turn their love for adventure into a real project. It all began with a single raft, a lot of enthusiasm, and the dream of sharing the excitement of rafting with people from all around the world.

Over time, and thanks to our commitment to safety, nature and delivering unforguetable experiences, the company grew. We went from offering small local trips to becoming one of the leading adventure tourism references in the region. Each of our guides is certified and has years of experience navigating rapids of all levels.
