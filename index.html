Chile

Chile is a beautiful country in South America. It is bordered by Argentina to the east, Bolivia to the northeast and Peru to the north. Chile has beautiful places, like "The Andes", "Torres del Paine" and "Easter Island". Santiago, Chile's capital city, has a lot of places to go visit. like "San Cristobal" hill, where you can see the entire city, surrounded by mountains.
